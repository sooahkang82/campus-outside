# Campus Outside

## Introduction
Campus Outside is an AI-powered service that helps college students instantly plan spontaneous outings. Just enter your current location, group size, and available time, and the AI generates a personalized course based on reviews shared by other students — covering route, estimated cost, and recommended spots. Completed plans can be shared directly via KakaoTalk.

## Key Features
- AI Course Recommendation: Generates a personalized outing plan based on location, group size, and time available
- Review Feed: Anonymous, student-submitted reviews and neighborhood rankings
- Student-Specific Tags: Context-aware tags like "great for a second hangout" or "good for large groups"
- KakaoTalk Sharing: Share generated courses instantly as shareable cards
